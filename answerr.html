THE IMPORTANCE OF A FAMILY

A family is ordained by GOD from the beggining in the book of GENESIS.
It is very important we embrace a family and take care of the family no matter the backround you have come from it.
No matter the situation the parents and brothers they are we should never forget them or refuse to help them in time of need.

As young men, when God blesses us we should not forget where we have come from,
for there is a blessing when we love and take care of our family and help them.

The background we come from should be our reminder to make us work hard and smart.
In order to succeed well in life we need to be putting GOD first in all things we do.
